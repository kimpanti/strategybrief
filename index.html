Before you begin
Please read and accept the following terms before using StrategyBrief.

Data processing & AI. This tool uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate strategy content. Any information you enter — including organisation details, competitive context, and strategic priorities — is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. By using this tool you consent to this processing. Do not enter classified, legally privileged, or personally identifiable information.

Data storage. StrategyBrief does not store, log, or retain your inputs on its own servers. However, Anthropic may retain API inputs and outputs in accordance with its Privacy Policy and Usage Policies. Review these policies before entering sensitive information.

Shareable links. The "Copy shareable link" feature encodes your strategy brief data in a URL using base64 encoding. Anyone with this link can view your brief. Do not share links containing confidential or proprietary information outside your organisation.

No professional advice. Content generated by StrategyBrief is AI-assisted and intended for brainstorming and structuring purposes only. It does not constitute legal, financial, investment, or professional strategic advice. Always engage qualified advisers for consequential decisions.

Accuracy. AI-generated content may contain errors, omissions, or plausible-sounding inaccuracies. All output should be reviewed and validated by qualified professionals before use in any official capacity.

Intellectual property. You retain ownership of the inputs you provide. Generated outputs are provided for your use, subject to Anthropic's terms of service.

Jurisdiction. This tool is made available without warranty. Use is at your own risk.
